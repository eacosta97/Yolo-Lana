Vindinium

What is this game called "Vindinium"?

The game Vindinium is an AI game, that is designed for you to create your own bot and to design him and give him the abilities to do whatever you want them to do. The attributes that your bot are given depend on you, wheter you want to make him an all out attack bot or a field dominant bot.

Playing Vindinium

When my classmates and I started to play vindinium it was all for fun, since we didn't relly now how to make our bots dominate other bots. However as we began to learn more about the bots and the coding vindinium became a more competitive game since we had our own server which had our own leaderbords.
